Zeus

Zeus is the god of the sky, thunder, and lightning. He is considered the king of the gods. He was born to Kronos and Rhea. He is the youngest of his 5 other siblings.

Poseidon

Poseidon is the god of the sea and possesses a powerful trident capable of conjuring up powerful storms. He is the older brother of Zeus and the son of Kronos and Rhea.

Apollo

Apollo is the god of the sun and light. He had the duty of riding his sun chariot everyday to bring the Sun to the mortals in Earth. He is the son of Zeus.
